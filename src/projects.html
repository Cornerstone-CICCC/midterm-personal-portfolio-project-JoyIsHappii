7van

A responsive website for 7VAN, a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy, designed to welcome newcomers and make joining simple. It highlights essential class and membership information in an engaging way—encouraging applications and supporting the growth of the 7VAN community. 🥋

IPPON

A dedicated social media platform built exclusively for karate practitioners — bringing together news, events, training resources, and a focused community in one place. Designed to cut through the noise of general social media, this app helps martial artists stay informed, sharpen their skills, and connect with others who share the same passion for karate. 🥋

Bunny World

A native iPad app created through the Design Sprint method, designed as an educational tool for children with learning disabilities. The project focuses on understanding their pain points to support skill development and make learning feel more comfortable and engaging. Long-term, the goal is for the tool to be trusted and recommended by professionals such as teachers, researchers, and doctors.
